Why Choose Us

High Service Quotient

Founded and run by young minds who have change the track of their life by only to help the underserved.

Open and Transparent

We provide to those who are in need and from those who want to donate. We are in direct contact with the famous restaurants and hotels all over the country, who want to share the basic necessity of living, food, with everyone.

Dedicated Goal

We aim to provide food to the needy ones so that no one has to sleep starving. We aim for a Hunger Free India where every individual should have access to healthy food.
